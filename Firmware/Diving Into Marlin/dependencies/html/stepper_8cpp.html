ISR ( TIMER1_COMPA_vect  )

Stepper Driver Interrupt

Directly pulses the stepper motors at high frequency. Timer 1 runs at a base frequency of 2MHz, with this ISR using OCR1A compare mode.

OCR1A Frequency 1 2 MHz 50 40 KHz 100 20 KHz - capped max rate 200 10 KHz - nominal max rate 2000 1 KHz - sleep rate 4000 500 Hz - init rate
